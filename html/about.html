YOUR EXPERT IN CONSTRUCTION AND RENOVATION IN MONTREAL!

Since 1989 Construction Daniel Dargis Inc. helps individuals, real-estate investors, promoters and general contractors with feasibility reports and field construction management for the construction and renovation of residential and commercial projects. For the individuals, first we will visit the sites and we make sketches, plans and estimates. Then, we manage the construction sites or we assist, supervise and help the general contractor that has been selected for the project. We help general contractors to get contracts and projects through referrals coming from the demands of our clients. In addition, for the entrepreneurs, real-estate investors and promoters we help them get and sell their lands and projects, do engineering and architecture drawings and to start in business through many services offered by the Construction Business Center in Montreal CONSTRUCTIONBUSINESSCENTER.COM. In Montreal we are the experts in construction and renovation.

1960 – 2010

Construction Daniel Dargis Inc. has been founded by Mr. Daniel Dargis, engineer, in 1989. The company represents the second generation of a long family tradition involved in construction projects since 1960. Between 1989-1997 the company has built new homes in Trois-Rivières, Quebec. During the same period Mr. Daniel Dargis, Eng., collaborated in the construction of the ABI aluminum plant in Becancour and Lauralco in Deschambault, Quebec. Between 2000-2006 the company has mainly specialized in the renovation of homes and shops in the region of Montreal doing kitchens, bathrooms, basements, home-movie theatres, balconies, home extensions and general reparations. Between 2006-2009 Construction Daniel Dargis Inc. moved to Alberta and Manitoba to participate in the management of the building of modular camps for oil and gas projects and also, at the same time, to do key-in-hand renovations of residential projects in Calgary.
